Why Become a Guide?

Improve English

Practice and enhance your English communication skills with international tourists

Get Certified

Earn volunteer certificates recognizing your cultural ambassador role

Global Network

Build connections with people from around the world

Leadership Skills

Develop valuable leadership and organizational abilities

Requirements

Eligibility

  • Currently enrolled university student
  • Based in Thanjavur region
  • Basic English communication skills
  • Knowledge of local culture and history

Time Commitment

  • Minimum 2 tours per month
  • Initial training program (2 days)
  • Monthly review meetings
  • Flexible scheduling around classes

Responsibilities

  • Guide tourists safely and professionally
  • Share accurate cultural information
  • Maintain communication standards
  • Report tour experiences

Training Program

Day 1: Orientation

  • Program overview
  • Cultural sensitivity training
  • Communication skills workshop
  • Safety guidelines

Day 2: Practical Training

  • Historical site visits
  • Route planning
  • Storytelling techniques
  • Emergency procedures

Ongoing Support

  • Mentorship program
  • Regular workshops
  • Performance feedback
  • Skill development
